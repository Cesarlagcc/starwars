Attack of the Clones

Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones is the second Star Wars film, and was released in 2002. It is the second part of the prequel trilogy which began with Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and ended with Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. The film is set ten years after the Battle of Naboo, when the galaxy is on the brink of civil war. Under the leadership of renegade Jedi Master Count Dooku, thousands of systems threaten to secede from the Republic.
